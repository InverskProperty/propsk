About Service Charge Statements

Service Charge Statements show the financial activity for a block property account:

  • Opening Balance - Balance at the start of the period
  • Income - Service charge payments received
  • Expenses - Block expenses paid (utilities, maintenance, etc.)
  • Closing Balance - Balance at the end of the period
