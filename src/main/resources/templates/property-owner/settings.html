About Billing Periods
What is a billing period?

A billing period determines when your rental month starts and ends. Most landlords use calendar months (1st-31st), but some prefer custom periods like 25th-24th.

Why does it matter?

Setting your default billing period ensures your statements are always calculated using the correct dates. This is especially important for pro-rating calculations when tenants move in or out mid-period.

Can I change it later?

Yes! You can update this setting at any time. You can also override it when generating individual statements.

Tip: Most property managers use the 1st (calendar months) for simplicity.
